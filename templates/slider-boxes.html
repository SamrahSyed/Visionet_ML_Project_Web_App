Pose Estimation

Human Pose Estimation is defined as the problem of localization of human joints (also known as keypoints - elbows, wrists, etc) in images or videos. It is also defined as the search for a specific pose in space of all articulated poses. Use cases include activity recognition (sick person falling down or security and surveillance), motion capture and augmented reality, training robots, fitness, retail virtual try-ons, and so on.
