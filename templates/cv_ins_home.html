Instance Segmentation

Instance segmentation is the task of detecting and delineating each distinct object of interest appearing in an image by finding out accurately the exact boundary of the objects in the image. It differs from semantic segmentation in the sense that it gives a unique label to every instance of a particular object in the image. Use cases include virtual try-on and make overs in retail industry, virtual image search, self-autonomous driving, virtual backgrounds for video conferences, medical imaging, and so on.
